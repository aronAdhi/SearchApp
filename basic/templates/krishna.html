Krishna

Krishna is a major deity in Hinduism. He is worshipped as the eighth avatar of the god Vishnu and also as the supreme God in his own right.

He is known for his mischievous pranks as a young boy, his role as a charioteer in the epic Mahabharata, and his teachings to the warrior Arjuna in the Bhagavad Gita.

Krishna is often depicted with blue skin, playing the flute, and surrounded by devotees.
